    Treat Team

  • Released Date: CBT in September 2022
  • My Role: Game Developer
  • Softwares Used: Unity, C#, Visual Studio, Git, Jira, Figma, Miro

    Project LIGHT: Prototype

  • Released Date: August 2022
  • My Role: Gameplay Programmer
  • Softwares Used: Unity, C#, Visual Studio, GitHub, Jira, Confluence

    • Project LIGHT: Prototype is an educational case study of Game: FTL-Faster Than Light, and heavily inspired by its game mechanics and system. It was developed by a total of 19 members during the summer of 2022. This prototype currently only has a shop and combat system with a single enemy ship at the moment. However, it will continue to be developed in the fall of 2022 and the full version will be released most likely at the end of this year. As a gameplay programmer, I've designed and constructed three different combat systems for both a player and an enemy. Documented every script that I've written with detailed examples as you can see in Unity Documentation, so that new incoming developers can comprehend code easily.
